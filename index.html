ℹ️ How Cross-Area Coverage Works
1. Area Manager reports an absence with no same-area cover available
2. They enable "Search other area" and select a cross-area replacement
3. The request appears here — the other area's manager must approve
4. Once approved, the replacement is confirmed and coverage history is updated
5. If rejected, the absence goes back to "Needs Replacement" status
